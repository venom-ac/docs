## Overview

Welcome to the public Venom Anti-Cheat documentation!

Venom Anti-Cheat, also known as VenomAC, is a FiveM anti-cheat solution that protects your server from modders, cheaters, and hackers.

You can find any and all information about our product here including an installation guide, configuration guide, documentation for our external Web and FiveM APIs for developers, and much more.
